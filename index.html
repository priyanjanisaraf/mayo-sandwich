Mayonnaise Sandwich Recipe!

Description-

Yummy and easiest recipe which you can make under 10 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Mayonnaise
  • Onions-2nos
  • Capsicum-1nos
  • Carrot-1nos
  • Half a cup boiled sweet corns
  • Oregano
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Black Pepper
  • Homemade coriander chutney (optional)

How to make it-

  1. Chop the vegetables mentioned above in the ingredients list. you can also add vegetables of your choice.
  2. Now add all the chopped vegetables in a mixing bowl.
  3. After adding vegetables add mayonnaise and mix them well.
  4. Now add salt, black pepper, oregano and chilli flakes according to your taste.
  5. Mix, mix and mix.
  6. Now takes two slices of bread apply butter on one bread and homemade coriander chutney on another.
  7. Apply the fresh filling you just made on the bread.
  8. Grease your pan with some butter.
  9. Cook your sandwich on low flame until crispy or brown.
  10. Serve it hot with ketchup or schezwan chutney.
